1st Year English Exam Paper Breakdown

Detailed breakdown of the objective and subjective sections.

Objective Part (20 MCQs)

In the objective part of the paper, there are 20 MCQs, and each MCQ carries 1 mark, making it a total of 20 marks. This section contains 3 subparts:

  • Mixed (Book I + Book II) MCQs: 10 synonyms
  • BOOK III (One-Act Plays): 5 MCQs
  • Choose the correct form of Verb: 5 MCQs

Total Marks: 20 Marks

Subjective Part (80 Marks)

Questions No. 2: (Marks: 6x2=12)

9 short questions from Book I (Short Stories). The students have to answer ANY SIX questions in 3-5 lines.

Questions No. 3: (Marks: 5x2=10)

8 short questions from Book III (One-Act Plays). The students must answer ANY FIVE questions in 3-5 lines.

Questions No. 4: (Marks: 4x2=8)

6 short questions from Book III (Poems). Students must answer ANY FOUR questions from the poem texts.

Question No. 5: (Marks: 10)

One question with internal choice (either an application or letter or the given topic).

Question No. 6: (Marks: 10)

Students must write a story on ONE of the two given moral lesson topics.

Questions No. 7: (A) (Marks: 5)

A stanza from any poem from Book III is given, and students must explain this stanza with reference to the context.

Questions No. 7: (B) (Marks: 5)

An unpunctuated paragraph from Book I short stories is provided. Students have to punctuate these lines according to the text.

Questions No. 7: (C) (Marks: 5)

7 pairs of words are given. Students have to use ANY FIVE pairs in their own sentences.

Question No. 8: (Marks: 15)

A paragraph from Book I (Short Stories) is given. The students must translate it into Urdu or, for English medium students, write an essay on the given topic.

Total Marks: 80 Marks

Tips for Objective Paper

  • Prepare from objective notes for English 1st year; almost all MCQs come from these notes.
  • Learn tenses and popular English usages.
  • Highlight important words and learn their synonyms from every lesson.
  • Study preposition notes for 2nd year English as they will help you for MCQs.
  • Never leave any answer blank. Always mark an option even if unsure.
  • For the objective paper, first attempt the easy questions to save time for difficult ones.

Tips for Subjective Paper

  • Start with the easy short questions from each section.
  • Keep track of time. Many students struggle with completing the paper on time.
  • Refer to the list of important stories to prepare efficiently.
  • Use Zahid Notes for 1st year English as they provide a great way to prepare.
  • If short on time, focus on the guess paper for 1st year English 2024 to secure a minimum of 60 marks.
